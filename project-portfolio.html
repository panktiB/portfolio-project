The Pretty Pictures

On the way to the base-camp from Haridwar-The holy city, we stopped to observe the holy spot of Devprayag, which is the confluence of the rivers Alaknanda and Bhagirathi. It is said that this is the spot where river Ganga was born.

Experience Yourself

During the trek we constantly saw beautiful trees and flowers and many birds as such, but the Rhododendron tree and its flowers are the most beautiful to look at. The tree has different colors in each season and at higher altitudes you get to see the other colors such as dark pink and light pink. These flowers are edible and have the tastiest juices in them but the light pink ones are said to be poisnous and should never be consumed.

Expereience Yourself

We reached the Chopta camp site, which is where we spent the last 3 days of the trek. We completed the summit and spent another day there, just to take in the atmosphere and appreciate the beauty that we never get to experience in the city.
